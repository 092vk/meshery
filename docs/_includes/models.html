What are Meshery Models?

Meshery Models are foundational components within Meshery, a cloud-native management plane that facilitates the management of multi-cloud and cloud-native applications and infrastructure. Meshery Models define a structured set of entities that Meshery can manage, offering a standardized way to represent and interact with cloud-native resources.
Each Meshery Model is based on a Model schema with well-defined attributes. This allows Meshery to maintain a consistent and extensible approach to representing infrastructure components and their relationships. Meshery Models can encompass simple applications, complex microservices architectures, and even infrastructure platforms like Istio or Nighthawk.
To effectively manage these resources, each model is supported by registrants, which are responsible for configuring specific infrastructure platforms, whether cloud-based or self-hosted. Registrants vary in their capabilities, offering everything from infrastructure-specific orchestration to additional functionality that extends Meshery's features.
